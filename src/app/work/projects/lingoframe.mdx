## Overview

Lingoframe is an AI-powered platform designed to make video content more accessible and engaging for everyone, everywhere. It simplifies the process of generating, customizing, and translating subtitles.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Subtitle Generation**: Uses advanced AI to listen and transcribe video content with high accuracy.
- **Multi-Language Translation**: Seamlessly translate subtitles into multiple languages to reach a global audience.
- **SRT & Burned-In Subtitles**: Options to export subtitle files or hardcode them directly into the video.
- **Real-Time Customization**: Fully customize the look and feel of your subtitles to match your brand style.

## Technologies Used

- **Node.js & Express**: Scalable backend architecture.
- **React & Next.js**: Fast and responsive user interface.
- **React & Vite**: Fast and responsive user interface.
- **MongoDB**: Reliable storage for user data and video metadata.
- **FFmpeg**: Video processing and manipulation.
- **Vercel**: Seamless and fast deployment.
- **Spaceship**: VPS for hosting the backend.
- **Cloudflare R2**: Object storage for video files.

## Outcome

Successfully launched with a credit-based system, empowering educators and marketers to localize their content with ease.
