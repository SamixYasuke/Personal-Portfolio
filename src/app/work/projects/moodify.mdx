## Overview

Moodify is a unique project designed to align your daily productivity with your emotional well-being. It helps you prioritize tasks based on how you feel.

## Key Features

- **Emotion Tracking**: Simple and intuitive interface to log your current mood.
- **Mood-Based Prioritization**: Suggests tasks from your backlog that fit your current energy levels.
- **Progress Tracking**: Visualize how your mood correlates with your task completion rates over time.
- **Intuitive UI**: A minimalist design that reduces cognitive load.

## Technologies Used

- **React & TypeScript**: Robust and type-safe frontend.
- **Vercel**: Seamless and fast deployment.

## Outcome

Provides users with a mindful approach to productivity, reducing burnout by encouraging a balanced workload.
