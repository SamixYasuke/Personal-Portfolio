## Overview

**The Player Rising** is a specialized research hub and digital case study platform. It explores how specific media narratives—like the "Starboy" or the "Natural Finisher"—shape the public perception and developmental trajectories of young professional footballers from top European academies.

## Key Features

- **Club-Centric Analysis**: Research categorized by elite clubs, including **Arsenal**, **Manchester United**, **Napoli**, and **Olympique de Marseille**.
- **Discourse Mapping**: In-depth deconstruction of media "framing" and its impact on a player's valuation and career pressure.
- **Data-Backed Insights**: Integrates mixed-media storytelling with quantitative career timeline data derived from professional scouting databases.
- **Narrative Search**: Interactive database allowing users to search by specific media labels to identify recurring patterns in sports journalism.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js & React**: High-performance frontend for a seamless, media-rich analytical experience.
- **Tailwind CSS**: Custom dark-themed aesthetic mimicking premium sports media outlets.
- **Node.js**: Backend logic for content categorization and data analysis.
- **Vercel**: Efficient CI/CD and hosting for a globally accessible academic project.

## Outcome

The project successfully bridged the gap between academic media study and modern sports journalism, providing a unique lens through which to view the next generation of football superstars.
